{: .mt-5 .mb-4 .border-bottom .pb-3 .font-weight-bold }

**Type:** Professional Product Development  
**Employer:** Par-Kan Company (Silver Lake, IN)  
**Product Model:** PK-CBC510 Collapsible Baggage Cart  
**Market:** Airport Ground Support Equipment (GSE)

Designed and developed a reengineered collapsible baggage cart for airport ground support operations. The PK-CBC510 is a **patent-pending** product that introduces a novel folding mechanism, enabling the cart to collapse for compact storage and dramatically reduced shipping footprint — a high-performance, cost-efficient solution for airline and ground handling fleets worldwide.

## Design Challenge
{: .mt-5 .mb-4 .border-bottom .pb-3 .font-weight-bold }

Traditional baggage carts used at airports are rigid, bulky structures that occupy large amounts of space when not in use and are expensive to ship in quantity. Par-Kan needed a redesigned cart that would:

> **📦 Reduce Shipping Costs** — Collapse to a fraction of the deployed volume for cost-effective bulk shipping
{: .block-tip }

> **⚡ Minimize Setup Time** — Allow rapid field assembly/disassembly without tools
{: .block-tip }

> **🏋️ Maintain Load Capacity** — Match or exceed the structural performance of conventional rigid carts
{: .block-tip }

> **🔒 Ensure Reliability** — Withstand repetitive folding cycles and harsh airport ramp environments
{: .block-tip }

- **GD&T & Tolerance Analysis** — Stack-up analysis on critical interfaces
- **DFM/DFA** — Designed for cost-effective fabrication and field assembly
- **Product Development** — Full lifecycle from concept through commercial release
