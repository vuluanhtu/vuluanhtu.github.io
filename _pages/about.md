I'm a Mechanical Design Engineer with a Master of Science in Engineering and 3+ years of professional experience in full-cycle product development. Currently working at Par-Kan Company, I specialize in designing Ground Support Equipment, Agricultural Machinery, and Waste Management Systems from concept through production.

My expertise centers on **modular system design** and managing large-scale assemblies with 1,000+ components. I'm a **patent-pending innovator** with experience developing novel mechanisms that have reduced customer setup time by 4x and shipping costs by 3x. I combine strong analytical skills in FEA/CFD simulation with practical knowledge of sheet metal DFM/DFA to optimize designs for manufacturing.

I hold an **FE Certification** and have published research in the MDPI Actuators Journal on modular self-reconfigurable robotic systems. My Master's thesis explored morphable robotics, demonstrating my ability to tackle complex engineering challenges through first-principles analysis and innovative design.

**Core Competencies:**

- **Design**: SolidWorks, AutoCAD, 3D Modeling (Top-down & Bottom-up), Kinematic/Dynamic Analysis
- **Analysis**: ANSYS, MATLAB/Simulink, FEA, CFD, Thermal Analysis
- **Manufacturing**: GD&T (ASME Y14.5), DFM, DFA, Tolerance Stack-up Analysis, Sheet Metal Fabrication, Structural Steel Weldments (AWS), Powder Coating, CNC Machining, 3D Printing
- **Integration**: Hydraulic/Electrical Systems, Motor/Drive/Actuator Sizing, Mechatronics
- **Programming**: Python, MATLAB, C/C++, Arduino
