Excited to announce that my patent application for an innovative thermal management system has been granted! This design addresses critical cooling challenges in high-performance electronics.

---

The patent covers a novel approach to heat dissipation using a combination of:

- **Phase-change materials** for thermal buffering
- **Optimized fin geometry** based on CFD analysis
- **Integrated heat pipe technology** for enhanced thermal conductivity

This technology has potential applications in:

#### Key Applications

<ul>
    <li>Electric vehicle battery cooling</li>
    <li>High-performance computing systems</li>
    <li>Industrial equipment thermal management</li>
    <li>Aerospace thermal control systems</li>
</ul>

The design was validated through extensive FEA simulations and physical testing, showing a **30% improvement** in heat dissipation compared to conventional solutions.
